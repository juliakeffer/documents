To re-run the Ingest process

At any time, you can configure the Ingest modules and rerun the Ingest process. You may to do this if you want to process the image for additional information that was previously not included in the Ingest process.

Under Images in the left panel, right-click on the image you want to process.

In the menu that appears, click Restart Ingest Modules. The Ingest module configuration window appears.

Choose the modules you want to include and click Start.

The Ingest process begins. You can monitor the progress in the bottom right corner of the main window. You can also view progress messages  by clicking on the icon in the toolbar at the top.
